FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

Most websites are delivered within 5–7 business days from the time we receive all your content and details. Larger projects like online stores may take 10–14 days.

Do I need to know anything technical?

Not at all! We handle everything technical. You just tell us about your business and we'll do the rest. We also explain how to make basic updates yourself after delivery.

Can I pay in Ugandan Shillings?

Yes! Ugandan clients can pay via Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel), bank transfer, or any other agreed method. We'll send you a quote in UGX.

What if I don't have a logo?

No problem. We can create a simple logo for you as part of the package, or design your site using your business name styled as a logo (wordmark). Just let us know when filling the form.

Can I request changes after delivery?

Yes — each package includes up to 3 revision rounds. After that, additional changes are billed at a small hourly rate. We make sure you're happy before finalising.
